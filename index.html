Love thy Neighbor

Yep —At CribScore we believe helping our fellow Americans is a good thing. If you are a user of Yelp, Google, CarFax or any other consumer-protection platform designed specifically to help others make well-informed decisions, then you will love CribScore. Its Renter Beware and Landlords be Smart! And yes, for the love of your home/income property --- we do need both.

CribScore is the first consumer-protection tool allowing landlords and tenants to finally have a true meeting of the minds about a rental when signing that lease agreement — Meaning each party is clear on the condition of that rental as livable according to state and local laws; even the implied laws most tenants are not aware of. See Implied Warranty of Habitability. No one likes surprises like mold, outdated electrical panels or toilets that do not work well; and sometimes we only get alerted to such problems by other tenants or people in the know.

That’s why CribScore’s focus is to provide the most up-to-date information on a unit so renters and landlords can focus on getting the problems resolved; which is good for the property and those who live there.

Using CribScore not only lets both both landlord and tenants wins, the house wins as well and that is smart land-lording
